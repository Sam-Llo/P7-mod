Beach Bonanza

Have fun under the sun in Beach Bonanza! Collect 3 or more of the Winning Symbol to win a prize. Uncover hidden Treasure Chests for instant wins! Grab a Fruit Drink to spin for wins in the Beachy Bonus.

How to Bet

Bet Settings Menu

Press the cog or TICKET COST to open the bet settings menu.

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

To change the ticket cost:

Select the desired ticket cost.

On desktop devices, optionally press the minus button (–) to decrease the ticket cost, or press the plus button (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press OK to confirm and close the bet settings menu.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

At the start of the game, the Winning Symbol for the game is revealed.

Press each Beach Ball to reveal symbols. Collect 3 or more of the Winning Symbol to win the prize displayed in the prize table. 1, 2, or 3 Winning Symbols can be found in a single grid space.

Reveal a Treasure Chest Symbol to instantly win the displayed prize amount. Multiple prizes may be awarded.

Reveal a Fruit Drink Symbol to trigger the Beachy Bonus game.

Beachy Bonus

The Beachy Bonus begins with 3 spins of the grid. Collect 5 or more Fruit Drink Symbols to win the prize displayed in the prize table. 1, 2, or 3 Fruit Drink Symbols can be found in a single grid space. If a Fruit Drink Symbol appears on the grid, the spins counter resets to 3.

The Beachy Bonus game ends when 16 Fruit Drink Symbols are collected or if no Fruit Drink Symbols appear for 3 consecutive spins. Only 1 Beachy Bonus game may be awarded.

The game ends when all symbols have been revealed and any triggered bonus game has completed. A message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.
